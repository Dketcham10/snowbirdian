Acquisition Criteria

What we look for

If your business fits most of these, we should talk. If you're not sure — reach out anyway. Every conversation is confidential.

Business Profile

Established & profitable

Stable operating history with consistent cash flow and a reason customers keep coming back.

Size

$100K — $1M+ in annual earnings

Flexible on size for the right business — owner earnings, SDE, or EBITDA.

Industries

Services, recurring revenue, niche leaders

Home services, B2B services, technology-enabled businesses, and durable local franchises.

Geography

Arizona & the Sun Belt — open to remote-capable

Based in Scottsdale, AZ. Businesses that can be operated or overseen from anywhere are welcome.

Situations

Retirement, relocation, new chapter

Owners ready to step back — whether fully exiting or staying involved through a transition.

Deal Style

Straightforward & seller-friendly

Clear terms, reasonable diligence, and structures that work for both sides — including seller financing where it fits.

The Process

From first call to close

A clear, respectful process designed to protect your confidentiality and your time.

Intro conversation

A confidential, no-pressure call to understand your business and your goals.

NDA & review

We sign an NDA, review high-level financials, and confirm mutual fit.

Offer & diligence

A written offer with clear terms, followed by focused, reasonable due diligence.

Close & transition

We close on schedule and work with you on a transition that sets everyone up to win.

Typical timeline: 60–90 days from first conversation to close.
